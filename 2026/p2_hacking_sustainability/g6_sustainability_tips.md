We introduce GreenTips, a cross-platform commandline interface (CLI) that provides developers with daily suggestions of general-purpose, sustainability oriented design patterns, along with additional resources for further learning. The tool aims to communicate sustainability principles in software development through a digestible, high-level format that supports the development of sustainability literacy among developers without imposing significant additional time demands.
